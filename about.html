About Us

Welcome to BES, your go-to destination for high-quality speakers and audio components. Based in France, we are passionate about bringing the best sound experience to our customers by offering an extensive selection of top-tier brands and DIY solutions.

For over 10 years, we have been committed to providing exceptional service, competitive pricing, and fast delivery. With a warehouse and a catalog of more than 14,000 products, we ensure that audio enthusiasts, professionals, and businesses have access to the finest speaker components available.

Leading Brands in One Place

We take pride in partnering with the best manufacturers in the industry, including: RCF, PHL Audio, Fostex, Kartesian, B&C Speakers, Mundorf, Peerless, Celestion, SB Acoustics, Lavoce, Eminence, BMS, Radian, Visaton, SEAS, Audax, Oberton, FaitalPRO, Sica, TangBand, and many more!

More Than Just a Website

At BES, we are more than just an online store. Our dedicated team is here to assist you with product selection, technical advice, and custom solutions. Whether you're a professional audio installer, a DIY enthusiast, or simply looking for the perfect speaker, we're here to help.
